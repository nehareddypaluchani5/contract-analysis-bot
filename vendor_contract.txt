VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement is entered into on 15 March 2024 between
XYZ Traders, Hyderabad and ABC Retailers, Chennai.

1. Scope of Supply
The Vendor shall supply goods as per the specifications agreed in writing.

2. Payment Terms
Payment shall be made within 90 days from the date of invoice submission.

3. Delivery and Penalty
A penalty of INR 10,000 per day shall be applicable for delayed delivery.

4. Termination
The Company may terminate this Agreement without assigning any reason.

5. Jurisdiction
All disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai.

6. Auto Renewal
This Agreement shall automatically renew every year unless terminated by the Company.
