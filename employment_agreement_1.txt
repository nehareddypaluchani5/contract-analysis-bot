EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made on 1 January 2024 between
ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd, Bangalore
and Mr. Rahul Sharma.

1. Term of Employment
The Employee shall be employed for a period of two (2) years starting from the joining date.

2. Compensation
The Employee shall be paid a monthly salary of INR 50,000 subject to applicable deductions.

3. Duties and Responsibilities
The Employee shall perform duties assigned by the Company from time to time.

4. Termination
The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without prior notice.
The Employee may resign by giving 60 days written notice.

5. Non-Compete
The Employee shall not engage in any competing business for a period of two years after termination.

6. Indemnity
The Employee shall indemnify and hold the Company harmless against all losses, claims, or damages.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
